From: ali@forkfox.ai To: partnerships@visitphilly.com Subject: ForkFox: dish-level data for Visit Philly's food coverage
Body (Paste Verbatim)
Hi Visit Philly partnerships team, I'm Ali, co-founder of ForkFox — an AI that scores individual dishes at Philadelphia restaurants. As of May 2026 we've scored 3,600+ dishes across Center City, South Philly, Fishtown, East Passyunk, the Italian Market, and surrounding neighborhoods.
A few ways I think this could be useful for Visit Philly
- Curated dish lists by neighborhood for visitor itineraries (e.g. 'top 5 dishes in East Passyunk under $25') - Data feeds for Visit Philly's editorial team - Co-branded content highlighting under-the-radar dishes at well-known restaurants
No cost, no commercial ask — we're in iOS TestFlight beta and the priority is getting the data in front of people who care about Philly food. Happy to share a sample dish-level dataset or hop on a 15-min call. Thanks, Ali Mabsoute ForkFox · forkfox.ai · ali@forkfox.ai
After Sending
1. Log in tracker → Press tab: outlet, date sent, status. 2. Set a reminder for 7 days from now to follow up if no response. 3. If they reply YES to a partnership conversation, schedule a 30-min call. 4. If they reply NO or with caveats, ask: "Is there a better time to revisit?" Sometimes "not now" means "not next quarter."
Why This Angle Works
Visit Philly is a tourism agency. Their KPI is visitor satisfaction + length of stay. Dish-level recommendations directly help both — visitors who order well have better trips and recommend the city to friends. Pitch is about THEIR audience benefiting, not ForkFox getting press.

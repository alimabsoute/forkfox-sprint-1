Project / Product Name
ForkFox
URL
https://forkfox.ai
Beta description
500 char max
ForkFox is an AI-powered iOS app that scores individual dishes — not restaurants. We use a patent-pending algorithm to read thousands of reviews per restaurant and score each dish on four axes: quality, value, execution, and consistency. 3,600+ dishes scored across Philadelphia and the San Francisco Bay Area. Beta users average 11-minute sessions (food apps average 2-3). Founded 2026 by Ali Mabsoute. Free iOS beta via TestFlight — invite arrives within 24 hours of signup at forkfox.ai.
Platform
iOS (TestFlight beta)
What testers will do
500 char max
Use ForkFox to find a dish to order at a real restaurant. Score 3+ dishes on the in-app rating. Tell us via email or in-app feedback: which scores felt accurate, which felt off, and what was confusing about the install or first-use flow. Sessions average 11 minutes — we want to know if your experience matches that.
Target audience
iOS users in Philadelphia or San Francisco Bay Area who eat out 2+ times per week. Foodies, app-comfortable users, ages 22-45.
Test duration
Ongoing — open beta until App Store launch (target: late June 2026).
Compensation
None — early access only. Founders read every piece of feedback.

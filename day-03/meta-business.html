Config step (NOT a submission) so Day 5 IG ads can launch

Sign in with: ali@forkfox.ai (the personal Facebook account that owns the Business Manager)

Business address
Use your home address OR Eagleville, PA (per memory) — same address as what you'd put on Crunchbase. Required for tax compliance even if you're pre-revenue.
Tax ID / EIN
Required for US businesses with payment thresholds above $600 lifetime ad spend. If you have an EIN already (Crunchbase + LLC paperwork), enter it here. If not, you can submit without an EIN initially — Meta will email if they need it once spend exceeds the threshold.

Limited Liability Company (LLC) OR Sole Proprietorship if no LLC formed yet.
Billing Setup
After saving Business Info, go to: Billing → Payment Methods. Add a card. Recommended: a card with a $20-$50/day spend limit you can manually set in your bank app, in case Meta misbehaves and overcharges.
Verification
Open Ads Manager. Look for any red banner saying "Account restricted" or "Missing business information." If clean, you're done. If still showing restrictions, click the banner and follow Meta's prompts — usually a one-time identity verification (driver's license photo).
Notes
Meta sometimes takes 24h to approve new business info. If Day 5 morning shows still-pending status, contact Meta Business Help via the chat icon (bottom right of Ads Manager) — typically 30-min response time.

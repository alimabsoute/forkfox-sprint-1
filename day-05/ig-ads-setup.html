Instagram Ads Setup Guide

Step-by-step launch guide ($10/day, Philly +25mi, iOS only)
Open this URL → https://business.facebook.com/adsmanager
Sign in with: the personal Facebook account that owns the Business Manager
Header
Goal: $10/day, 2 ad variants, iOS-only Philly +25mi, traffic objective, Stories + Reels + Feed placements. PREREQUISITE: Day 3 Meta Business info filled — verify Ads Manager shows no red restriction banner before starting.
Step 1 — Campaign Create
1. Open Ads Manager. Click "+ Create." 2. Buying type: Auctions (default). 3. Campaign objective: "Traffic" (NOT Conversion — pixel needs data first). 4. Campaign name: "ForkFox Philly iOS Sprint W1" 5. Special Ad Categories: None (uncheck if Meta auto-checks one). 6. Click "Continue."
Step 2 — Campaign Budget
1. Campaign budget optimization: ON 2. Daily budget: $10.00 3. Campaign bid strategy: Highest volume (default). 4. Click "Continue."
Step 3 — Ad Set / Targeting
1. Ad Set name: "Philly iOS Foodies" 2. Conversion location: Website 3. Performance goal: "Maximize number of link clicks" 4. Website URL: https://forkfox.ai
5. Audience → Location
- Add: Philadelphia, PA + 25 mi radius - Excluded locations: none
6. Audience → Age: 21 - 40 7. Audience → Gender: All
8. Audience → Detailed Targeting
ADD ALL of these (interests): - Restaurants - Foodie - Cooking - Yelp - OpenTable - Eater - Bon Appétit (magazine) - Dining out
9. Audience → Languages: English (US) 10. Placements: MANUAL (NOT Advantage+) UNCHECK: - Facebook (we want IG only) - Audience Network - Messenger LEAVE CHECKED: - Instagram > Stories - Instagram > Reels - Instagram > Feed - Instagram > Explore 11. Devices: iOS only (UNCHECK Android — CRITICAL) 12. Click "Continue."
Step 4 — Creative: Variant A
1. Identity: ForkFox Instagram account (link if not already) 2. Format: Single image 3. Image: Upload _assets/images/ad_creative_A_1080x1080.jpg
4. Primary text
125 char max
Find the best dish near you in 5 seconds. AI ranks 3,600+ dishes across Philly + SF.
5. Headline
40 char max
AI ranks the dish, not the place
6. Description: leave blank 7. CTA button: "Learn More"
8. Website URL
https://forkfox.ai/?utm_source=ig_ads&utm_medium=cpc&utm_campaign=phl_w1_a
9. Click "Publish" to save Variant A as draft.
Step 5 — Duplicate For Variant B
1. In the Ad Set, click "+ Create Ad" or duplicate Variant A. 2. Image: Upload _assets/images/ad_creative_B_1080x1080.jpg
3. Primary text
Stop scrolling Yelp. AI scored 3,600+ dishes — know what to order before you sit.
4. Headline
Free iOS beta · Philly + SF
5. Website URL
https://forkfox.ai/?utm_source=ig_ads&utm_medium=cpc&utm_campaign=phl_w1_b
6. Save Variant B.
Step 6 — Launch
1. Both ad variants under one Ad Set means Meta will A/B test them automatically using its budget allocation. 2. Click "Publish" at campaign level. 3. Meta reviews in 1-24 hours (usually 2-4 for new accounts).
Step 7 — Don'T Touch For 72 Hours
Same as Reddit. Both variants need 72 hours of impressions before judgment.
If Something'S Wrong
If ads show "In Review" longer than 24h
- Click the campaign → click any ad → check for review issues - Common rejections: text > 20% of image area (Meta no longer auto-rejects but flags low-quality), missing landing page disclaimer, ad copy too promotional.
If CPCs above $3 in first 24h
- Audience is too narrow OR competitive (Philly + iOS + Foodie is a premium segment).
Either: Expand Radius From 25Mi To 35Mi, Or Drop One Of The Lower
converting interests.
Targeting Notes
iOS-only is critical. Without it, ~40% of impressions go to Android users who land on a "no Android version" message and bounce — wasted spend. The "Eater" interest specifically targets Eater readers. They're our exact persona — food-engaged, neighborhood-aware, willing to travel for a good dish.

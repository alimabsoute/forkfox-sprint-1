ForkFox is an AI-powered iOS app that scores individual dishes at restaurants — not the restaurants themselves. We use a patent-pending algorithm to read thousands of reviews per restaurant, extract dish-level signal, and score each dish 0-100 on four axes: quality, value, execution, and consistency. 3,600+ dishes scored so far across Philadelphia and the San Francisco Bay Area. The score is personalized — your 94 won't match your cousin's 94, because palates differ and ForkFox learns yours. Real example: 47 ramen bowls scored across 12 Philadelphia restaurants. We tell you which broth, which noodle, which neighborhood. Beta users average 11-minute sessions (most food apps average 2-3 minutes). Currently iOS-only, free TestFlight beta — invite arrives within 24 hours of email signup at forkfox.ai. Founded 2026 by Ali Mabsoute and Nik.
